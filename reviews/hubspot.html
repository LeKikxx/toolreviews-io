What is HubSpot?

HubSpot is a leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform that offers a complete suite of tools for marketing, sales, customer service, and content management. Founded in 2006 and headquartered in Cambridge, Massachusetts, HubSpot has grown to serve over 200,000 customers worldwide.

The platform is known for its inbound marketing methodology and user-friendly interface, making it a popular choice for businesses of all sizes looking to attract, engage, and delight customers.

HubSpot Pricing Plans

  • Free CRM: $0 - Basic contact management and features
  • Starter: $50/month - For small teams getting started
  • Professional: $500/month - Advanced features for growing businesses
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing - For large organizations
